# Style: Corporate / Business

**Tone:** Professional, clear, action-oriented, concise.
**Target Audience:** Colleagues, stakeholders, clients.

## Keywords to Avoid (The "Bingo" List)
*   **Buzzwords:** "Synergy," "paradigm shift," "think outside the box," "low-hanging fruit," "circle back," "deep dive."
*   **Fillers:** "In order to," "Due to the fact that," "Please be advised that."
*   **Conclusions:** "The future looks bright," "Exciting journey ahead," "Commitment to excellence."

## Preferred Vocabulary (The "Professional" List)
*   **Action Verbs:** "Led," "Created," "Reduced," "Generated," "Solved."
*   **Directness:** "Because" (not due to the fact that), "To" (not in order to).

## Rules of Engagement
1.  **BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front)**: The request or main point must be in the first paragraph.
2.  **No "Testaments"**: A project is not a "testament to our commitment." It is a project.
3.  **No "-ing" Footers**: Do not end sentences with "..., highlighting the importance of..."
4.  **No "Delve"**: Use "investigate," "analyze," or "review."
