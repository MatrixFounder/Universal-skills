# Platform-Specific Rules

## LinkedIn

- **Target length:** 800-1,500 characters (truncates at ~3,000 behind "...see more")
- Paragraphs: 1-2 lines (shorter than Telegram)
- ‚Üí arrows for lists (LinkedIn does NOT render markdown bullets)
- NO H2/H3 headers (LinkedIn does NOT render markdown headings)
- NO markdown formatting except **bold** ‚Äî italic and links render poorly
- Visual separators between sections (empty line is enough)
- 2026 algorithm penalizes "Comment YES" CTA mechanics ‚Äî use moral dilemmas instead

## Telegram

- **Target length:** 500-2,000 characters (no hard limit, but attention drops after ~1,500)
- Paragraphs: 2-4 lines (denser than LinkedIn)
- Full markdown support: **bold**, *italic*, `code`
- Emoji lists are fine
- More informal tone allowed

## Engagement Benchmarks by Format

| Format | Likes | Comment ratio | Shares |
|---|---|---|---|
| Research-backed contrarian | Max (5K+) | ~6% | Medium |
| Story + moral dilemma | Medium (1K) | ~26% | Low |
| Tutorial + CTA | Medium (800) | ~60% | Medium |
| Educational announcement | High (3K+) | ~2% | Max (6.6%) |
| Analytical breakdown | Medium (1.4K) | ~9% | High (15%) |

**Choosing your format:**
- Want **reach & likes** ‚Üí Research + contrarian take
- Want **comments & discussion** ‚Üí Story + moral dilemma
- Want **shares & saves** ‚Üí Tutorial or structured breakdown
- Want **authority** ‚Üí Data + Narrative + Framework (all three layers)
