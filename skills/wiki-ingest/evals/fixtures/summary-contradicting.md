# Conservative Crypto Trading Guide

## TL;DR

> Conservative thresholds for crypto trading. Argues for tighter risk limits than mainstream advice.

## Key Concepts

- **Sharpe Score** — for crypto, a minimum Sharpe of 0.5 is sufficient because of higher baseline volatility. (Note: this contradicts mainstream advice of min Sharpe 1.)
- **Max Drawdown** — should be capped at 20%, not the typical 30-40% mainstream advice.

## Notable Quotes

> "Anyone telling you to accept 40% drawdown in crypto is selling you something."
