# Cold Email — Quality Criteria

Anchors: 50 = average, 70 = good, 90+ = exceptional.

## Dimensions (total: 100)

### Hook & relevance (30 points)
- Opens with something specific to the recipient, not a generic greeting (0-15)
- Reason-for-reaching-out is clear in the first two lines (0-15)

### Concrete value (25 points)
- States a tangible, believable outcome (number, timeframe, named result) (0-15)
- Avoids vague superlatives ("revolutionary", "best-in-class") (0-10)

### Brevity & scannability (20 points)
- Under ~120 words; short paragraphs; one idea per line (0-12)
- No filler sentences that could be deleted without loss (0-8)

### Single clear ask (15 points)
- Exactly one low-friction call to action (0-15)

### Tone & credibility (10 points)
- Confident, human, not pushy or salesy; no spammy phrasing (0-10)
